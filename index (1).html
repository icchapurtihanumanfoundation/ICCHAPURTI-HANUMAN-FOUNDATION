Temple Story

According to the villagers, the Icchapurti Hanuman Mandir is a place where countless devotees have found peace, strength, and the fulfillment of wishes. Legends say that Lord Hanuman himself blessed this site, making it a powerful center of devotion for over five centuries. The temple walls have witnessed generations of prayers, and with renovation, it will continue to bless many more to come.
