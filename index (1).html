ğŸ“– Icchapurti Hanuman Temple Story

The divine journey of this temple began long ago, with Hanumanji being present on Indrayani Fort since 1831.

In **1978**, Hanumanji appeared in the dream of a farmer, **Shri Laxman Dagadu Jadhav**, with a divine instruction:

"I am situated at a height of 1300 feet on Indrayani Fort. Build my temple so that devotees can worship me."

Following this divine call, Shri Jadhav, along with **Shri Kashinath Jadhav** and **Shri Bhavrao Jadhav**, installed the idol from the fort under a sacred tree in IndraiWadi.

Initial construction began in **1998**, but unfortunately, due to a lack of funds, the project could not be completed, leading to the current dilapidated state.
