About Icchapurti Hanuman Foundation

We are a non-profit community trust working to renovate and protect the ancient Icchapurti Hanuman Mandir at Indraiwadi, Post Vadbare, Chandwad, Nashik. This temple has been a source of faith and blessings for generations. The renovation helps preserve our cultural and spiritual heritage.
