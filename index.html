About the Foundation

Icchapurti Hanuman Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to renovating and maintaining ancient Hanuman temples. Our current focus is the Icchapurti Hanuman Mandir at Indraiwadi. The restoration includes structural repairs, painting, puja area improvements, and basic amenities for devotees.
