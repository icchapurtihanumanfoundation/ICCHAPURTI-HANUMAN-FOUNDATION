About Us

We are a registered non-profit trust Icchapurti Hanuman Foundation, located at Indraiwadi, Post Vadbare, Chandwad, Nashik. Our mission is to restore and preserve ancient Hanuman temples, keeping alive the spirit of Sanatan Dharma and devotion for generations to come.

The Divine Story of Icchapurti Hanuman Mandir

According to local tradition, the Icchapurti Hanuman Mandir was established nearly five centuries ago. It is said that a wandering saint, blessed with divine vision, discovered a powerful Hanuman idol in the forests of Indraiwadi. With the support of the villagers, a shrine was built at the very spot, which over the years became a sacred center of devotion.
